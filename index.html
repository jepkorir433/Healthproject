Finnish Diabetes Risk Score (FINDRISC)

Risk Score = Age + BMI + Waist Circumference + Physical Activity + Diet + Medication + History + Family History

Scoring: 0-8 points = Low risk | 9-14 points = Moderate risk | 15-20 points = High risk | >20 points = Very high risk

Validation: 78% sensitivity and 77% specificity for predicting type 2 diabetes within 10 years
